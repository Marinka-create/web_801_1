Sights of Georgia

Georgia is an amazing country where ancient traditions are harmoniously intertwined with modern life, and majestic mountains are adjacent to cozy seaside resorts. Traveling here, you can enjoy the exquisite Georgian cuisine, visit the picturesque ancient monasteries and fortresses, taste the famous local wines.

Every corner of this hospitable country is ready to give unforgettable impressions: from bustling Tbilisi to secluded mountain villages, from the azure coast of the Black Sea to the snow-capped peaks of the Greater Caucasus.

  •     A hospitable country with famous Georgian traditions of cordiality and hospitality
  •     Mountain Pearl — majestic peaks of the Caucasus and picturesque valleys
  •     Wine capital — ancient winemaking traditions and famous vineyards
  •     Culinary paradise — unique national cuisine and aromatic spices
  •     Natural contrasts — from snow-capped peaks to blooming gardens
